About the Game

Boom Merge: Mahjong Match Tiles I invites you to a world of tile-matching excitement! With an innovative twist on traditional mahjong, players merge matching tiles to create explosive combos and progress through beautifully designed levels.
Experience a blend of strategy, logic, and relaxation as you unlock new boards, discover themed tiles, and challenge yourself with progressively difficult puzzles. Whether you’re a casual gamer or a mahjong master, there’s something here for everyone.

How to Play

  • Match Tiles

    Select identical tiles to remove them from the board and earn points.

  • Merge for Power

    Combine tiles to trigger powerful explosions and clear large sections of the board.

  • Use Boosters

    Utilize tools like shuffles and hints to overcome challenging levels.

  • Complete Objectives

    Meet specific goals, such as clearing all tiles or achieving a target score, to progress.

  • Unlock Rewards

    Earn coins and stars to customize your boards and unlock exclusive themes.
